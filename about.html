About Our Company

SPDK Travel Agency is a web based online bus reservation system which is developed as a final year project by the students of Seventh semester at AIMS College (CSIT). In this system a person can book their tickets online by visiting our site. This web based system requires the login of the customer and provides the sophisticated experience to the customer for booking tickets and make their travelling a fun ride. This site provides the detail information about the seat availability, route and time required to reach the destination. This system provides the user with the feature of booking tickets, cancel reservation and check status of their reservation. This system also provides the user with the running status of the buses with full security.
